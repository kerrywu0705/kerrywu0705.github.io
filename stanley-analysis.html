YouTube Channel Performance Analysis

2022–2024 | Shorts Transformation Strategy | 1,785 Videos Analysed

Interactive Dashboard

Why This Project?

As a content performance analyst for a gaming YouTube channel with 500K+ subscribers, I was tasked with evaluating whether a strategic shift toward Shorts would drive subscriber growth and improve audience engagement — without cannibalising long-form video performance.

YouTube Analytics provides surface-level metrics, but it doesn't offer the cross-format comparative analysis needed for strategic decisions. I built this end-to-end analytics pipeline to answer the key business questions behind the Shorts transformation.

01

Is the channel's overall viewership growing or declining?

02

Do Shorts drive higher engagement than long-form videos?

03

Which champion topics generate the most views?

04

What is the optimal publishing schedule?

05

Are Shorts contributing to subscriber conversion?

Key Findings

Long Videos Remain the Core Revenue Driver

While Shorts make up 37% of uploads, long-form videos contribute over 82% of total views. The audience clearly prefers in-depth content, making long videos the foundation of the channel's value proposition.

1,130 Long Videos Analysed
655 Shorts Analysed
82% Views from Long Videos

Shorts Outperform in Engagement

Despite lower absolute view counts, Shorts consistently achieve higher engagement rates than long-form content. This validates Shorts as a tool for audience interaction rather than raw viewership.

2.05% Shorts Engagement Rate
1.52% Long Video Engagement Rate
+35% Shorts Engagement Advantage

Subscriber Growth Accelerated Post-Shorts

After ramping up Shorts production in mid-2023, subscriber growth rate increased significantly. The channel grew from 330K to 459K subscribers over the analysis period, with the steepest growth coinciding with the Shorts push.

330K → 459K Subscriber Growth (2022–2024)
+39% Total Subscriber Growth
~1.2% Average Monthly Churn Rate

Technical Overview

Data Collection

  • YouTube Data API v3
  • 1,785 videos (2022–2024)
  • Filtered to ≤20 min duration

Data Processing

  • Python (requests, csv, json)
  • Champion name matching via lookup table
  • Long/Shorts classification by duration

Metrics

  • Engagement Rate, CVR, Churn Rate
  • Net subscriber growth modelling
  • Content-type CVR estimation

Visualisation

  • Chart.js (embedded, no CDN)
  • Standalone HTML dashboard
  • Interactive filters (year/month)
